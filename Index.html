About MakeMyBooks

Knowledge, Trust & Insight — Going Further

MakeMyBooks was built on a simple belief: small businesses deserve the same quality of financial infrastructure that large enterprises take for granted — without the overhead.

We are a team of Chartered Accountants and CPAs who specialise in digitising accounts, tax, and compliance for small and growing businesses. From cloud bookkeeping to real-time financial dashboards, we replace manual processes with smart, scalable systems that work while you focus on building your business.

Our approach is always tailored. We listen, respond quickly, and deliver solutions that are technically sound, practical, and built to grow with you.

Strategic Leadership

Sahhil Chawla

Strategic Advisor

Sahhil brings a rare combination of financial acumen and technology fluency to MakeMyBooks — bridging the gap between traditional accounting practice and the digital tools that modern businesses need to compete.

His focus lies in enabling the Virtual CFO model for small businesses: conceptualising the right technology stack, identifying tools that eliminate manual work, and designing financial operating systems that give founders real-time visibility over their numbers — without needing a full finance team.

From ERP selection and cloud accounting migration to Power BI dashboard architecture and process automation frameworks, Sahhil shapes the technological and strategic direction that underpins every MakeMyBooks engagement — ensuring clients don't just get compliant books, but a genuinely smarter business.
